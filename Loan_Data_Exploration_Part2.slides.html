Dataset Overview¶

This document explores a dataset containing 113,937 loans with 81 variables on each loan, including loan amount, borrower rate (or interest rate), current loan status, borrower income, and many others.

what kind of person is most likely to take a loan and for how long? (Employment Status and Duration)¶

Most of loans are only taken by th employed status.

Employment Duration is inversely proportional with the number of loans (most of loans are less than 150 months)

The distribution of the borrower's Annual Percentage Rate (APR)¶

The distribution of APR has a large peak around 0.2, then a smaller one around 0.28

Also, there is a very high number of loans (more than 7000) with APR between 0.34 and 0.36.

What is the commeon Loan amounts used? (The distribution of Loan Original Amount)¶

Most of the Loan Amounts are multiples of 5000: ( 5000 - 10000 - 15000 - 20000 - 25000 )

What is the most used Monthly Loan Payment?¶

The distribution of Monthly Loan Payment has a large peak around 250, then a smaller one around 500, then a verry small peak around 850

Also, there is a very high number of loans (more than 11000) with Monthly Loan Payment between 170 and 180.
